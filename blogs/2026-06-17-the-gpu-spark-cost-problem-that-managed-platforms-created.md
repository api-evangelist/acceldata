Managed platforms like Amazon EMR add service markups on top of already-expensive GPU compute, amplifying the cost of GPU-accelerated Spark workloads. The article advocates self-managed Kubernetes deployments running NVIDIA RAPIDS Spark on customer-controlled EC2 GPU infrastructure, positioning Acceldata's xLake as a control plane that enables GPU-accelerated Spark without per-unit compute charges.
